  • Real-World: Decentralized Exploration Task Assignment Attack
    Robot 3 (the furthest robot) launches the attack. As a result, exploration goals cannot be distributed to the other two robots, and the task cannot be finished.
  • Real-World: Centralized Exploration Task Assignment Attack
    Robot 3 (the furthest robot) launches the attack. Due to the number of frontiers to explore is more than one, the other two robots may still receive the goal, yet not correctly updated.
  • Real-World: Map Merging Attacks
    For map merging attacks, the result is similar to the video shown in Decentralized Task Assignment Attack. Robots cannot move due to false map in centralized system; they cannot merge the map correctly in decentralized system.
